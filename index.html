The Team

Porter Killian

Porter is the founder of Saber Racing and a current senior at Corona del Mar High School, where he is on the high school sailing team. He has been sailing ever since he can remember and has sailed a myriad of boats, from Sabots to CFJs to Eliot 7 Meters and Swedish Match 40s. He has a true passion for the sport and plans to continue to sail as an adult. He also enjoys computer science and designed this website.

Jeffrey Petersen

Jeffrey is a sophomore at Mater Dei High School. He grew up racing sabots at the Balboa Yacht Club and has since sailed in a plethora of boats ranging from lasers to large keel boats in PHRF regattas. He is truly dedicated to the sport and is constantly looking for ways to improve.

Cameron Wood

Sailing has always been a part of Cameron Wood's life. He fell in love with the sport at the age of five, when he began making his way through sabots and eventually started sailing larger boats. His sailing career took off when he started sailing CFJs and won his first national championship in (YEAR). He went on to win the Keel Boat National Championship for Corona del Mar in (YEAR), as well as several other regattas along the way.

Robert Garrett

Born and raised on the water in Southern California, Robert has spent most of his life sailing ever since he was seven years old. After starting off in sabots, he then proceeded onto CFJs and lasers throughout middle and high school and finally into J70s and match racing. He is at home on the bow of just about any boat.

About Us

We are a Southern California-based match racing team. We compete regularly from coast to coast in the United States in boats ranging from 20 to 40 feet. We are currently ranked 98th in the world and plan to continue to work hard to be the best that we can be. All of the members of the team have a true passion for the sport and strive to be the best they can.
