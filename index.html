The Team

Porter Killian

Founder of Saber Racing a current Senoir at Corona Del Mar High School, where he is on the high school sailing team. He has been sailing ever since he can remeber and has sailed a myriad of boats, from Sabots to CFJs to Eliot 7 meters and Swedish Match 40s. He has a true passion for the sport and plans to continue into the distant future. He has also enjoys computer science and designed this website.

Jeffrey Petersen

Jeffrey is a current sophomore at the Mater Dei High School. He grew up racing sabots at the Balboa Yacht Club and since then has sailed in a plethora of boats ranging from lasers to large keel boats in PHRF regattas. He has a true passion of the sport and is constantly looking for ways to improve.

Cameron Wood

Sailing has always been a part of Cameron Wood's life. Even when he was five he was in love with the sport. He moved his way through sabots, and eventually started sailing big boats. His sailing carrear took off when he started sailing CFJs and won his first national champoinship. Later on he went on top win the Keel Boat National Champoinship, for Cornona Del Mar, and many other events.

Robert Garrett

Born and Raised on the water in Southern California, Robert has spent most of his life sailing since he was 7 years old. After starting off in sabots, he then proceeded onto CFJs and lasers throughout middle and high school and finally into J70s and match racing. He is at home on the bow on just about any boat.
