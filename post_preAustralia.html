Australia Round 2

Saber Racing is pleased to announce that we will be going down to Australia in early November to race in both the Harken and the Musto Regattas. The team that will be going down to the regattas is Porter Killian, Jeffrey Petersen, Cameron Wood, and two Australian girls from the Royal Prince Alfred Yacht Club, Emma Rankin and Juliet Costanzo/ Sarah Parker( for the Musto).

Both the Musto and the Harken are incredibly prestigious and it is an honor for us to receive invitations to these events, in fact 30 people applied for an invite to the Harken. Many of the people we will be racing against will be in the top 100 and the majority will be in the top 200. The Harken starts the 22nd of November, grade 3, will be raced at the Royal Prince Alfred Yacht Club in Eliot 7 Meters. We are hoping to improve on last years place of 9th and break into the top eight. The Musto starts the 27nd of November, grade 2, will be raced at the Cruising Yacht Club Of Australia in Eliot 7 Meters.
